Lord Studley Dudley

Height: 5' 10"

Weight: 175

Hometown: Atlantic Ocean? or Westchester, England

Entrance Music: Big Shot by Billy Joel

Finishing Manuever: Dirty South Slap

Championships Held: None

Bio: Formerly representing the United Kingdom and known as Lord Studley Dudley, he became tired of representing the snooty aristocracy and betrayed his motherland. He now claims to be Lowly Southern Dudley, representing the Dirty South. Born unexpectedly during a trip in the English Channel (Chunnel), he has lived similarly to his birth -- completely unpredictable. His father, Billy Bob, was a drunken hick who abused him, and his mother, in contrast, was a proper English businesswoman named Claire. He left his home three years ago for the States to start a new life. He has found this new life in the EWE. His peculiar and random behavior has become renowned by all his competitors. Known for his perseverance and determination, he even competed for nearly a year with a torn ACL. But he says himself he doesn't want that to be the main aspect of his wrestling career. He wants to be known on top, with the coveted EWE belt around his waist in triumph.
