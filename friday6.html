Pandemonium 2000-08-15

Pictures Below

This is the first Pandemonium since the Desperate Measures PPV and is just 3 days away from Midnight Massacre. We all know that this Pandemonium would have a Tag Team Title Tournament.

Out came the Victory Alliance with Winner, Credible, and the Commish JJ Clauson. Winner said how smart he was by saying that he was going to get some aluminum in the tourney. Clauson reminded Winner that Clauson was the Comissh, not Winner. He then said "I'm in control!!!!!!" and booked himself and Credible in the finals which mad Winner mad. It becomes worse win Credible told Winner that Gateway would not be here tonight and he had to find a partner. He went searching and found The Garbage Man. He reluctantly took him as his partner.

The first match was booked by the evil Victory Alliance in which they made the for members of the Down South Gang fight each other. Casey Tibbs, The Champ, came out by himself. Where was Victor E. Moore??? Tibbs spoke and told of the fact Victor E. would not be there. He needed a partner. All of the sudden a truck pulled up and out came T-Bone!!!!!!!!!! If you are not familiar, T-Bone was kicked out of the ECWF for lack of showing up, but to everyone's surprise he was here. T Squared was reborn. Still they had to face Russ Rage and The Franchise. Now in this match the special guest ref was The Commish JJ Clauson. The match started off rough. Finally Chris Winner came out of the back and The Franchise diverted his attention to Winner. He had left Rage in the ring alone to get his ass kicked by T Squared. Rage was pinned right when Franchise realized what he had done. T Squared moved on in the tourney.

The next match was Winner and The Garbage Man vs. White Trash Dudley, in his TV debut, and the Assman, the ECWF World Heavyweight World Champ. Although Winner and TGM were not a good team they made some good double team moves. At one point Assman through a legal object at Winner at it hit him where the sun don't shine, causing Winner to grimace in pain. The Franchise came out to distract Winner but Winner was too quick and got the pin after holding Assman so Clauson could hit him. This was Clauson making up for taking Winner out of the finals!!!! This set up tension between Assman and the Commish, sparking their match at the PPV. So now Winner and Garbage Man had advanced.

The next match was going to be a classic as Winner and TGM face T Squared. T Squared showed their tag team strength as the hurt the Garbage Man. Franchise came out and this time Winner could not ignore. Franchise split Winner's head with a steel chair!!!!!!! Winner came back and got the advantage on Franchise but soon realized that he and TGM had lost the match. T Squared would face The Commish and Dustin Credible in the finals.

So here we were in the finals of the ECWF Tag Team Title Tournament. A true Tag Team, T Squared, had fought 2 matches already, and now had to fight the Commish, JJ Clauson, and the ECWF North American, or as he likes to call it, North Alberta, Trophy Holder, Dustin Credible. The match started with the Victory Alliance getting a strong advantage with Winner as the ref. Every ECWF competitor was a lumberjack in this match. The match was going great until Assman decided to get Clauson back. Assman hit Credible, hit Clauson, and hit Winner. T Squared did their patented T Squared leg-drop and Assman counted 1, 2, 3. There was new ECWF Tag Team Title Holders in T Squared!!!!!!!!!!
