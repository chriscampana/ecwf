Victor E. Moore

Height: 5' 9"

Weight: 161 lbs.

Hometown: The Okeefenokee Swamp

Entrance Music:More Human Than Human - White Zombie

Finisher Setup: Figure 4 Leglock

Finisher: Arabian Facebuster

Quoted: "I hate you. You are not funny. I am funny. I rule and Chicks dig Vic."

Championships Held: This guy's a contender

Bio: Never seen without his trusty steel chair which he uses in every match. A very high flying wrestler. Makes claims to be a Lord of the Ladies. His adrenaline and/or hyperactivity is always sure to slow down the competition. No one seems to befriend this maniacal beast, but when he needs a hand someone always seems to help out. Referring to himself in third person is a common trait of Victor E. Moore and so is claiming sanctuary behind his Goliath idol, The Franchise. Will not stop his destruction until The Franchise has the world title. When asked why he simply replies, "Why Not, and then finishes with,"Hairy bears for life." The federation doesn't see to think he has all his screws, but then again does anyone in the federation have all of them?
