Midnight Massacre 2000-10-08

Pictures Below

This PPV started off strange. Chris Winner was shown preparing for his match with the Franchise when Dustin Credible came up to him. Now Dustin Credible and JJ Clauson have been on thin ice with Winner, and Credible told Winner that Clauson was not here. Winner thought it was great but then JJ called Credible's cell phone. JJ told Credible that the match between JJ and Assman for the World Tit

le was now going to be between Credible and Assman. Winner was steaming because he wanted the title shot. He told Clauson "screw you." Now the first match was for that belt between Assman and Credible. Credible was already the NA Champ and now going for the World Belt. They came out swingin' and Credible went for a Centon-Credibomb to start the match. Assman got up and hit Credible with a chain link chain. Then Assman smacked Credible with a chair shot from hell!!!!!!!!!!!!!!! It was crazy! Nevertheless, Credible was down but not out. Assman went for the Death From Above but Credible reversed it into a crazy DDT!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!! He locked Assman into the Canadian Maple Leaf and Assman passed out!! We had a new champion!!!! After that, Winner came into congratulate Credible and then promptly staple gunned his forehead, busting him open!!!!!!!!!!!! What's going on in the Victory Alliance???

Credible denounced the NA trophy so now Gateway vs. Tibbs would be fighting for it. T-Bone came out with his partner, Casey Tibbs, while Winner came out with Gateway. This match was a slober-knocker, until finally Tibbs hit the Triple LegDrop. When he went for the pin he got to a two count and T-Bone interuppted the pinfall!!!!!!!!!! He turned on Tibbs!!! Gateway got up and gave Tibbs the Jersey Driver to win his first title, the NA Title!! Then Winner hit Gateway. Winner and T-Bone were a team!!! They had this all planned!!!! Oh my God!!!

Now the interviewer explained what just happened but was hit in the back of the head by Winner, who explained that he and T-Bone are two parts of a trio that will take the ECWF by storm!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!! Who is the third person?????

The next match was singles in T-Bone with Winner vs. White Trash Dudley, who had just explained to the world that his real name is Lord Studley Dudley, and that he is from England and related to Prince Charles. On top of that, the Assman came and said that is is LSD's brother, "Gary Big Hands Dudley." He must have been still dazed from that beathing by Credible. Anyways in the match T-Bone used help from Winner to get the upper hand on Dudley. But Assman--er, Gary Big Hands Dudley, helped LSD. Winner tried to help T-Bone but GBHD gave Winner the Death From Above and the Dudleys gave T-Bone a 3D for the win.

The next match was for the tag titles since T Squared were no longer champs. It was a three way match with the Dudleys vs. Gateway and Tibbs vs. Winner and T-Bone. All six men were in the ring at one time for most of the match. Finally GBHD locked Winner in FATTMISSION and would not let up. When he finally did, Winner beat him down. GBHD mustered up enough energy and and hit the Death From Above, but Winner feel on top of him and got the pin so T-Bone and Winner were tag champs!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!! After the match Tibbs and Gateway stole the titles.

The main event was Winner vs. The Franchise. It started off with a test of strength with Winner first getting the upper hand but Franchise came back. Winner then tried to call time out and when Franchise went for him he received an eye gouge from Winner. Then Winner hit the Buff Blockbuster on Franchise. Franchise got back up and gave him the Simon series, a series of two suplexes and a facebuster. Winner then but Franchise in a sleeper that he got out of. Winner started working on the right leg. He elbowed the knee many times of Franchise. Then he gave Franchise Enziguri and both men were down from exhaustion. They got up and Winner recieved a Franchiser but kicked out!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!! Winner went for the Tiebreaker but Franchise kicked out!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!1 Winner then did a Winner Slam and fell on top for the 1, 2, 3 win.
