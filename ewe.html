EWE Event 2002-06-03

Pictures Below

The was the first PPV in a while and appropriately named "the first ever EWE event". The event happened mysteriously as Chris Winner only called the event to tell the EWE stars about his changes as commish.

The show started with Lawrence O'Malley and the Canadian Dream Dusty Orth running down the show's schedule, even though they didn't even know who was going to appear. They talked about how Christopher Winner had obtained rights to the ECWF from JJ Clauson, the former commish, and that he had changed the name to EWE. Since all the titles had been stripped in November, there would be all new champions tonight.

Next was Chris Winner interview time in front of the EWE banner. He had a new shirt that said Commish Chris Winner on the front and "oh yeah, you're fired" on the back. He said that since all the belts had been stripped there would be a battle royal for the World title tonight. Winner also said he had fired Russ Rage for biting off Winner's mom's ear!! Then Franchise came out and he didn't want Russ Rage fired. He challenged Winner to one on one match and if Winner wins, the Winner is commish, but if Franchise wins, then Russ Rage is reinstated into the EWE. Then Winner and Franchise brawled into the backstage area.

The first match started right after LSD came out and talked. He said from now on his will be known as Lowly Southern Dudley, the most Southern man in EWE. It was fitting that he was fighting Victor E. Moore, another kid from the confederacy in EWE. The fought a long battle until LSD gave Moore the Dirty Southern Slap in which he licks his hand, reaches it down his pants, and slaps his opponent. Moore was out but LSD would not get off of Moore. LSD had snapped and the referee reversed the match and made Moore the winner. Now Moore is the number one contender for the North American title, which was being fought for next.

The North American title match was Assman vs. Dustin Credible vs. Casey Tibbs. This was a long fought match. Assman went out first when he tapped out to the 7 seconds of glory by Casey Tibbs. The Credible did the Credibomb and Tibbs was out giving Credible the NA Title.

The next match was Winner vs Franchise with the stipulations mentioned above. This was a long fought match but it was won when Winner used two low blows on Franchise then performed the Winner slam and won. Winner then stuffed pieces of Russ Rage's contract down Franchise's throat meaning Russ Rage was fired!!!

The next match was the battle royal for the EWE World Title. The first man out was the mysterious Amigo Doz. He left as mysteriously as he came in. Who is this Amigo Doz. Where was his partner? Then LSD was eliminated by Victor E. Moore. Then Victor E was eliminated by Tibbs who was eliminated by Credible. The Assman came in and destroyed everyone. He came in wearing all leather on a harly davidson with rock music in the background. Winner and Franchise got him out and soon Franchise beat Winner to win the EWE belt. Now Franchise was EWE champion.
