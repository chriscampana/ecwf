Desperate Measures 2000-07-21

Pictures Below

This was one of the greatest PPVs in ECWF history and the first PPV on the new ring. Because White Trash Dudley was a no-show then Russ Rage would be fighting the Garbage Man and Casey Tibbs would have Dustin Credible in a singles match for ECWF North American Title. The show started with Christopher Winner, JJ Cluason, and Dustin Credible sitting in their locker room. Gateway was not there because of injury. Clausoon had just gotten back from his tour of South East Africa, where they love the ECWF. He was very surprised and angry to learn that Christopher Winner had given The Franchise another chance in the ECWF. JJ then said how he gave Credible a shot at the North American belt which displeased Winner, as he wanted all the spotlight.

The next segment featured an in-ring interview with the Victory Alliance. They reviewed the card and said how the Alliance will have all the gold in the ECWF, even the world title match, which a mystery person chosen by the Victory Alliance. Then on to the fist match.

The first match was The Garbage Man vs. former ECWF NA Title holder Russ Rage. Russ Rage hit the Off The Rope Demolisher for the first time off ropes!!! He controlled most of their match until The Garbage Man called for the garbage can. But Rage blocked the can and hit the Sexy Slam for the 1, 2, 3 and sort of an easy win for Rage in The Garbage Man's comeback.

The next match was for the ECWF North American Title. It had The Champ Casey Tibbs vs. Dustin Credible. In the beginning of this match we had the debut of the North American Title Trophy, not a belt. The match started off rough with Casey Tibbs using close fisted punches to get Credible off his feet. All of the sudden Credible went for a Centon-Credibomb which Tibbs moved out of the way. Soon after that, Tibbs went for a Hurricanrana but Credible blocked it and locked Tibbs in the Lion-Tamer where Tibbs was forced to submit. Credible was the new ECWF North American Title Trophy holder!!!!!

The third match was still a mystery to everyone. Who would Assman be fighting? We would soon know as Assman came out to his PERMANENT theme music, Assman by James Johnston. Soon we heard a loud "yee, haw." What was this? Then we saw. Not from the entrance way but from the woods came none other than Victor E. Moore!!!!!!!!!!!!! No one put it together but Victor E. had to be part of the Victor-y Alliance. So the match started with a much pumped Victor E. destroying Assman. But Assman's experience soon took over as he hit Moore with a choke-slam. He then went for an off the rope move but was hit in the back with a chair by Chris Winner!!!!! Moore gave Assman the Arabian RibBuster but Assman kicked out. Assman then hit his finisher, the implant DDT, and Moore was out. Credible and Winner came in to help out Moore but the mood turned quickly sour as the Victory Alliance took apart Moore for not winning the belt for the Victory Alliance. This was a shame.

The main event of the day was Christopher Winner vs. The Franhise for Franchise's contract in the ECWF. JJ Clauson hated the Franchise and wanted him out of the ECWF so he would obviously interfere in this match. Winner came out with the contract and burned it to imply of the "firing" of The Franchise. This match started off physical with The Franchise hitting many offensive moves. Then Winner locked a sleeper on The Franchise and it looked like he was out but Franchise got back up. Then Franchise hit the Triple Diamond, in which he hits two suplexes and a facebuster. Winner low blow'd the Franchise and brought him to the roper where The Commish threw a blinding liquid in the face of the Franchise!!! Winner the hit the Winner Slam but Franchise kicked out!!!!!!!!!!!! Winner brought him to get another dose of the liquid but Franchise moved and it hit Winner!!!!!! Winner was temporarily blinded and grabbed the first body he felt which was JJ Clauson!!!! He threw Clauson into the ring. There JJ tried to tell Winner it was him but Winner Winner-Slammed Clauson!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!! Then The Franchise gave Winner the Franchiser and locked him in the Crippler Crossface!!! Winner would not give up and eventually passed out due to the pain. Franchise has won!!!!!!!! He was back in the ECWF!!!!!!!!!! Back in the ring, a new Alliance was starting as The Franchise, Victor E. Moore, Russ Rage, and Casey Tibbs all destroyed the Victory Alliance. Winner received a Sexy Slam, Arabian RibBuster and another Franchiser. Oh my GOD!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
