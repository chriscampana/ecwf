Franchise

Height: 6' 1"

Weight: 185 lbs.

Hometown: Detroit Rock City

Entrance Music: Walk - Pantera

Signature Moves: Five Star Splash

Finishing Move: The Franchiser (Modified Stunner)

Quoted: "I never claimed to be a showstopper, I'm the F'n Show!"

Championships Held: Current Three time ECWF Heavyweight Champion (1/1/00-1/15/00) (3/25/00-5/6/00) (1/9/01-current)

Bio: The Franchise started the ECWF revolution when he declared himself the ECWF World Heavyweight Champion in early 2000. A one time friend, Chris Winner, stabbed his former best friend in the back when he attacked him from behind one day in the locker room and stole his ECWF gold. For some unknown reason the ECWF Championship committee honored Winners championship reign, so The Franchise had to challenge his former ally. At "Hard Time" The Franchise defeated Winner in the mega hyped "Table Death Match 2000" to win back what was rightfully his.

During this process The Franchise made an alliance with long time friend Russ Rage. The Franchise was a mentor to Rage and taught him much of his ring knowledge. "The Whole F'n Show" and "The Down South Kid" formed a tag team known as "The Eliminators" and have had many run-ins with the evil Victory Alliance.

The Franchise is a former 2 time World Heavyweight Champion and master technician. He knows a thousand holds, but he can match power with anyone. He isn't afraid to leave his feet and take it to the air either. His finishing maneuver "The Franchiser" is a deadly combination of jawbreaker and neckbreaker and is feared around the ECWF. He truly is The Franchise of Backyard Wrestling, he is The Backyard Icon, He is "The Whole F'n Show".
