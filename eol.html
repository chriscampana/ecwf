End of the Line 2000-12-03

Pictures Below

The first match of the card was the Tag Team Battle Royal with the current champs being T-Bone and Chris Winner. They won.

Now it was on to the former friends T-Bone vs. Tibbs. The match started off with a shoving match, when suddenly Tibbs gained the upper hand with viscous punches to the face. Tibbs body slammed T-Bone and tried for the leg drop to end the match quickly. T-bone would not go down that quickly though. When he realized what was happening he quickly rolled out of the way, then jumped to his feet and quickly took advantage. Delivering multiple kicks to the ribs, and went for his move the Elbow Drop and since no one no it was his move Tony Shiavone showed up with a chorus of "That's his move!!!!" But T-bone didn't go for the pin; instead he climbed to the top of the ladder, and then took a leap of faith, as he went for a big splash. Unfortunately for T-bone, Tibbs managed to roll out of the way, he then delivered the leg drop for the 1,2,3.

The Assman vs. Commissioner JJ Clauson (If Assman wins he is commissioner for whoooooo 90 days.)

Assman wasted little time for JJ to hit the ring. As soon as the commissioner's music started to play, Assman quickly jumped out of the ring, and went after him. JJ was prepared though, just as Assman came in view of Clauson, JJ swung and cracked him over the head with a chair. Clauson tried to pick the big man up, but he couldn't lift him so he just kicked him while he was on the ground. Finally, Assman slowly started to come to, and as he did, JJ started to run towards the ring. As soon as Assman got up, he began to give chase. The moment JJ saw this, he began to run around and around the ring, with the Assman slowly catching up. Clauson made a critical mistake because he jumped back in the ring to try and mount some kind of offense. Assman rolled into the ring and ducked under blinding liquid and gained the 1,2,3, by giving him death from above. He becomes Commish for Whooooo 90 days!

Now Dustin Credible vs. The Franchise. This match was a tie. There will be a rematch.

The Fire Match was won by Chris Winner, but that PPV sucked, time for the new PPV Burnin' Down the House
